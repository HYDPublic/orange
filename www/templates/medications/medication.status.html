Activating a medication will add it to your daily adherence schedule. You will be able to log doses of this medication. This is best for medications you are currently taking.

Pausing a medication will remove it from the daily adherence schedule. You will still be able to log, as needed doses. This is best for medications you are not taking for a short time.

Archiving a medication will remove it from the daily adherence schedule. You will not be able to do log doses without activating the medication again. This is best for medications you are no longer taking.

Deleting a medication will completely remove it from the daily adherence schedule. You will not be able to log doses without activating the medication again. This is best for medications you are no longer taking.
