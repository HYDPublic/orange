Logs are used to store all your medication information in one place.

You can securely store your health information in each log. Use them to save your:
  • ⏰ Medication Schedules
  • 📅 Dosing Records
  • ✏️ Health Notes
  • 👩‍⚕️Care Team
  • 💊 Pharmacies

We've created two logs to get you started: Jon Berry's log and Jon Berry's Personal log. You can create other logs for yourself, a family member, or someone you care for.
